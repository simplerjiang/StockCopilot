# GOAL-013 Step 2.3 Refinement Report (2026-03-13)

## EN
### Scope
- Refined the Step 2.3 delivery so market news no longer depends on selecting a stock first.
- Removed the query-history truncation and kept the full history visible through a scrollable layout.

### Development
- Backend:
  - Added `EnsureMarketFreshAsync` to `LocalFactIngestionService` so market refresh can run independently of symbol refresh.
  - Updated `/api/news` to allow `level=market` requests without `symbol`.
  - Added `QueryMarketAsync` to `QueryLocalFactDatabaseTool` for a symbol-free market bucket response.
- Frontend:
  - `StockInfoTab.vue` now fetches market news on mount even before a stock is selected.
  - Stock/sector buckets continue to wait for a selected symbol, but market requests are isolated and no longer fail them.
  - History chips now render the full sorted list and switch to a scrollable grid instead of `slice(0, 10)`.
- Tests:
  - Added backend coverage for market-only local fact queries.
  - Added frontend coverage for market news loading without a stock and for rendering more than 10 history items.

- 前端：
  - `StockInfoTab.vue` 现在会在页面挂载时先拉取大盘资讯，即使还没选股也能看到顶部条带内容。
  - 个股/板块 bucket 仍然跟随选股，但大盘请求已与它们解耦，不会再因为缺少 `symbol` 一起失败。
  - 查询历史改为渲染完整排序列表，并用可滚动网格替代 `slice(0, 10)` 硬截断。
