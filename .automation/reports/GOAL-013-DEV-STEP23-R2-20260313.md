# GOAL-013 Step 2.3 Rerun Report (2026-03-13)

## EN
### Planning
- Goal: redo Step 2.3 against the latest reviewer directive by removing the blocked Eastmoney sector-search dependency, enriching market-level news with both overseas and domestic sources, and making market news visible outside the AI sidebar.
- Source validation completed before coding:
  - Sina sector search HTML returned 200 and exposed usable `h2 > a` plus `.fgray_time` metadata blocks.
  - WSJ US Business RSS returned successfully in the current environment.
  - NYT Business RSS is still kept as a source, but the implementation tolerates timeout/failure and continues with the remaining feeds.
  - Sina roll macro feed remains reachable and is now merged into market facts instead of being only a last-resort fallback.

### Development
- Backend ingestion changes:
  - `LocalFactIngestionService` now fetches sector news from Sina search HTML (`https://search.sina.com.cn/?q={sector}&c=news`) instead of the blocked Eastmoney search API.
  - Added `SinaSectorNewsSearchParser` using `HtmlAgilityPack` with support for result-box markup, fallback heading-only markup, and Chinese relative/absolute publish times.
  - Market-level ingestion now aggregates `WSJ + NYT + Sina roll` and writes the merged result set into `LocalSectorReports` with `level=market`.
- Frontend changes:
  - `StockInfoTab.vue` keeps stock/sector fact buckets in `CopilotPanel`, but moves market news into a dedicated terminal-side card so it stays visible even when AI is collapsed.
  - The new market card reuses `/api/news?level=market`, supports refresh, scroll, sentiment tags, and outbound links.
  - Follow-up refinement: market news is now rendered as a standalone floating banner above the whole stock terminal instead of living inside any card.
- Tests updated:
  - `LocalFactIngestionServiceTests`
  - `SinaSectorNewsSearchParserTests`
  - `RssMarketNewsParserTests`
  - `StockInfoTab.spec.js`
