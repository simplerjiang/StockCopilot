  - Follow-up root-cause fix: the real blocker was not only rendering style but also the render-plan aggregation step globally applying `uniqueSortedNumbers(...)` to every indicator's `calcParams`, which silently corrupted ordered params such as `KDJ [9,3,3]` into `[3,9]` and turned `D/J` series into `NaN` at runtime.
